## Description — English

Trad is a satirical idle game about Swedish forest politics.

Choose your path: build a forestry empire as Silva Maximus, or nurture your woodland as Smaskogsagaren (the Small Forest Owner). Click, automate, and navigate bureaucratic obstacles while balancing production against biodiversity.

Features:
- Two unique game paths with different strategies
- Idle mechanics — your forest grows even when you're not playing
- Procedural forest visualization with falling leaves and fireflies
- Knowledge panel with real facts about Swedish forestry
- Bureaucratic brutalism aesthetic with millimeter paper grid

No account required. No ads. No in-app purchases.
